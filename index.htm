Introduction

To speech enable your website we need to talk to our WearHF service

 

What is WearHF

WearHF is an insightful new approach to running Android applications. It is essentially a system service, buried into the heart of the Android operating system that sits in-between the Android OS and its applications. 

WearHF is designed to automatically examine all applications at runtime, extract information about user controls as they appear on the screen, and translate these into hands-free actions such as speech or head-gesture controls. All of this happens seamlessly, at lightning speed, and without the application developer having to know anything about speech recognition or gesture engines. All buttons, menus, checkboxes and other clickable user interface components that appear on a screen are automatically interrogated and their names extracted at runtime. These names are fed into the always-on speech recognizer.

Installation

 

How to install and configure your HTM-1 device to run the developer preview

1.       Open ‘RWExplorer’

2.       Install ‘Keyboard-Developer.apk’ and ‘WearHF-Developer.apk’ by dragging it into

3.       After installing each apk would will receive a successful notification.

4.       Go to setting page by clicking the settings icon in RWExplorer.

5.       Next click on ‘Langage & Input’.

6.       Next click ‘Current Keyboard and then choose keyboards.

7.       Next toggle ‘Speech Keyboard’ off and Toggle ‘Speech Keyboard Developer’ on

8.       Navigate to the main settings page and click Accessibility

9.       Next click WearHF and switch the service Off. Click ‘ok’ on the dialog box that pops up.

10.   Next navigate back and click ‘WearHF Developer’ and switch the service on . Next click ‘ok’ on the dialog box that pops up

 

11.   Download our web browser from https://realwear.box.com/s/ehrgnrwb2bw1lnl9lbknrsysgfonnnll

 

12.   Once downloaded install the ‘browser.apk’

 

13.   Navigate to ‘http://configure.realwear.com/test’
